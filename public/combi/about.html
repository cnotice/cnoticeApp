Ace Your Memory. Master Your Learning.

Welcome to cNotice, a powerful and simple-to-use spaced repetition web app designed to help students retain information more efficiently and for the long term.

We built this tool with one goal in mind: to make studying smarter, not harder. Spaced repetition is a proven learning technique based on the science of memory — it helps you review information at the optimal time, just before you're about to forget it. This leads to stronger memory retention and less time spent cramming.

Whether you're preparing for exams, learning a new subject, or keeping up with coursework, our system schedules personalized review sessions to make your study time more effective. No more guessing what to revise — just show up and start learning.
